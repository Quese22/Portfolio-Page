In a world where everything is seemingly created and made for use, I have always wondered how I could lead?
How I could create, create something that could reverse some of the harm done? Always wanting to express my creativity
in a way that was for the benefit of us, the people. I remember creating drawings for cities that automatically
sheltered the homeless, suits that rehabilitaed the body after injury, and translators to help us communicate in times
of crisis. Engineering didn't work out, but my ambition to create never died. My want to make helpful technology is alive now more than ever.
In the face of climate change, mass shootings, and a lack of focus on mental health and education my focus to develop technology that helps
the common (wo)man to a higher level of conciousness and health, is alive now more than ever. Beyond a salary, the intent to make us all better.
The ownership, knowledge and freedom that IT brings me is all icing on the cake.
